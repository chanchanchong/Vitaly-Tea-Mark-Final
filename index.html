Eco-friendly

Creating safe, stunning wall decorations, suitable even for sensitive environments and public buildings such as schools and hospitals.  HP Latex Inks and PVC-free Wall Paper prints meet a range of stringent human health criteria: 

• HP Latex Inks have achieved UL ECOLOGO® and GREENGUARD GOLD Certification.
• HP PVC-free Wall Paper printed using HP Latex Inks is GREENGUARD® Children & Schools Certified.
• HP PVC-free Wall Paper is  Forest Stewardship Council® (FSC)  certified.
• GREENGUARD GOLD Certified HP Latex Inks print on HP PVC-free Wall Paper, helping building owners obtain LEED credits.
• Create wall graphics and prints that meet AgBB criteria and are A+ rated according to Émissions Dans L'Air Intérieur.
